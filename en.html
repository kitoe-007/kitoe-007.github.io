About

Sculpture, graphics and digital design combined into one language.

I am Daniil Gauss, an artist and designer with an academic background in sculpture and experience working with brands, media and cultural institutions. I combine sculptural thinking, graphic design and UX/UI to create visual systems with a strong character.

I am interested in materiality, texture and space. Digital objects behave like physical ones - they have weight, depth and mood. From tactile surfaces to interfaces and neural art, everything serves one goal - to build a cohesive visual language around a project.

Skills

From print graphics and identity to interfaces and digital objects.

Graphic Design

Identity, logos, brand systems, posters, covers, typography, composition. I work with print and digital formats, building systems that live both in media and in physical space.

UX/UI Design

Landing pages, promo sites, services. Structure, scenarios, Figma prototypes, visual architecture of the interface. I care about both logic and the tactility of the visual layer.

Neural Art and Digital Sculpture

Work with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, 3D and digital sculpture. I use AI as a tool, not as a replacement for taste. Focus on form, volume and the feeling of a physical object even on screen.
