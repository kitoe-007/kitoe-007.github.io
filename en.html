About me

Sculpture, graphics and digital design united in one language.

I am Daniil Gauss, an artist and designer with an academic background in sculpture and experience working with brands, media and cultural institutions. I bring together sculptural thinking, graphic design and UX/UI to create visual systems with character.

I am interested in materiality, texture and space. Digital objects behave like real ones: they have weight, depth and mood. From tactile surfaces to interfaces and neuroart, everything serves one goal—to build a cohesive visual language around the project.

Skills

From print graphics and identity to interfaces and digital objects.

Graphic design

Identity, logos, brand guidelines, posters, covers, typography, composition. Working with print and digital formats, building systems that work both in media and physical space.

UX/UI design

Landing pages, promo sites, services. Structure, user flows, Figma prototypes, visual interface architecture. I care about both logic and the tactile quality of the visual layer.

Neuroart and digital sculpture

Working with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, 3D and digital sculpture. I use AI as a tool, not as a replacement for taste. Focus on plasticity, volume and the feel of a physical object even on screen.
